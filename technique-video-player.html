GO BEHIND
From the front headlock position, circle to the back to take control. One of the most high-percentage finishes in wrestling — the front headlock is only as good as your ability to get to the back from it.
⏱ 45 sec
🎯 Offense
📊 +10 XP
🔑 KEY POINTS
›
1
Snap and circle — don't try to go straight behind. Circle pressure breaks their base and creates the opening.
2
Chin stays on their back — if your head comes off, you lose control. Head pressure keeps them down.
3
Lock hands behind the elbow — not around the waist yet. Control the arm first, then transition to waist control once you're behind.
4
Feet never stop moving — dead feet = dead position. Keep your feet active the entire time you're circling.
📋 COACH'S NOTE
Focus on the circle pressure this week. We drilled this Tuesday — rewatch before Thursday's practice. This is your #1 finish from front headlock.
